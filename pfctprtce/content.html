Inplant Training

At Vertisize Solutions, we are inviting teams of interested students for a 3-day in-plant training program. The in-plant training takes place during the month of September (Mon-Wed of every week).

The slots are filled on a first come first served basis. Interested students may enrol their names in batches.

Schedule:

Day 1:
  • Version Control overview
  • Introduction to Git
  • Linux overview
Day 2:
  • Project Development overview (includes Requirement collection, Data modelling, API design, etc...)
  • Cloud services overview
Instructions:
  1. 5 students are allowed per batch.
  2. Bonafide certificate / College ID card is required for enrollment.
  3. Attendance is mandatory for all the three days of the program.
  4. Students must bring their own laptops.
  5. A fee of Rs.3,000 / person will have to be paid on the first day.
Certification:

Participation certificates will be provided to all students on the final day. A 100% attendance in the 3-day program is mandatory for certification.
